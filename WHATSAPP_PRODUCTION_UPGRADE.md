- [ ] Business address proof (utility bill in business name or owner name)

- [ ] Any ONE of:
  - [ ] Udyam Registration

  - [ ] Trade License
  - [ ] Professional Tax Registration
  - [ ] Business website with domain registration
  - [ ] Business bank account statement

**For Other Countries:**
- Check Meta's requirements for your country
- Usually: Business registration + Tax ID + Address proof

## 💰 Pricing (Production Mode)

**WhatsApp Business API Pricing:**

### Conversation-Based Pricing

**Business-Initiated Conversations:**
- When YOU send first message (like booking confirmation)
- Charged per 24-hour conversation window
- India: ~₹0.40 - ₹0.80 per conversation

**User-Initiated Conversations:**
- When customer messages you first
- First 1,000 conversations/month: FREE
- After that: ~₹0.20 - ₹0.40 per conversation

**Template Messages:**
- Count as business-initiated
- Each template message starts a 24-hour window
- Multiple messages in 24 hours = 1 conversation charge

**Example Costs (India):**
- 100 booking confirmations/day = ~₹40-80/day
- 1,000 booking confirmations/month = ~₹400-800/month
- 10,000 booking confirmations/month = ~₹4,000-8,000/month

**Note:** Prices vary by country. Check Meta's pricing for exact rates.
