## 📝 Verification Process

### Step 1: Start Verification

1. Go to: https://business.facebook.com/
2. Click: **Business Settings** → **Security Center**
3. Click: **Start Verification**

### Step 2: Choose Verification Method

**Select: "Upload Documents"**
(Instant verification won't work without GST/business registration)

### Step 3: Fill Business Information

**Business details:**
```
Business Name: airlinecabz
Alternative Name: Airlinecabz (optional)
Business Website: https://airlinecabz.com
Business Phone: +91 99013 66449
Business Address: [Your operating address]
```

**Business Tax ID/Registration:**
```
Leave blank or enter: "Not Registered"
```

**In the text box, explain:**
```
We are a small transportation service business operating through our website airlinecabz.com. We do not have GST registration yet as we are below the threshold. We are providing personal identification documents and domain registration proof for verification.
```

### Step 4: Upload Documents

**Document 1: Personal ID**
- Upload: Aadhaar Card (front and back as separate files)
- Label: "Business Owner ID Proof"

**Document 2: PAN Card**
- Upload: PAN Card
- Label: "Tax Identification"

**Document 3: Address Proof**
- Upload: Utility bill or bank statement
- Label: "Business Address Proof"

**Document 4: Domain Registration**
- Upload: Domain registration email/certificate
- Label: "Website Ownership Proof"

**Document 5: Website Screenshot (optional but recommended)**
- Take screenshot of your homepage showing business name
- Take screenshot of contact page
- Combine into one PDF
- Label: "Business Website Evidence"

### Step 5: Add Explanation

**In the additional information box:**
```
Business Name: airlinecabz
Website: https://airlinecabz.com
Business Type: Transportation/Cab Service
Operating Since: [Year you started]

We operate a legitimate cab booking service through our website. We are a small business currently below GST registration threshold. 

Documents provided:
1. Aadhaar Card - Business owner identification
2. PAN Card - Tax identification
3. Utility Bill - Business address verification
4. Domain Registration - Website ownership proof

Our website is fully functional with booking system, contact information, and privacy policy. We are requesting WhatsApp Business API access to send booking confirmations to our customers.

Contact: +91 99013 66449
Email: [your email]
```

### Step 6: Submit

1. Review all information
2. Click: **Submit**
3. Wait for Meta's review (1-5 business days)
